**How to Use the AI Resume Checker**

Step 1: Click [Telegram bot](https://t.me/Fayaz_Resume_checker_bot)

Step 2: Start the bot and send the message "Hi".

Step 3: The bot will ask you to submit your resume link.

Step 4: Please open your resume in [Google docs](https://docs.google.com/) (Resume file must be docs).

        Important Steps:
        1️⃣ click share on right top corner.
        2️⃣ Set access to “Anyone with the link – Viewer”.
        3️⃣ Copy the link.
        
Step 5: Paste the resume link in the Telegram chat.

Step 6: Wait for 2–5 minutes while the resume is analyzed.

Step 7: The resume analysis report will be **sent to the email address mentioned in your resume**.
